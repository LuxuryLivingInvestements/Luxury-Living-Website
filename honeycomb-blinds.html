ENERGY EFFICIENT HONEYCOMB BLINDS

Luxury Living Swakopmund introduces premium Honeycomb Blinds, designed to combine modern style with excellent thermal performance. The unique cellular design creates insulating air pockets that help improve indoor comfort throughout the year.

Available in Palma Range 25mm and Palma Range 45mm, these blinds provide elegant light control, privacy and a clean contemporary finish suitable for residential and commercial interiors.

Honeycomb Blinds are an ideal choice for coastal homes, bedrooms, living areas and spaces where energy efficiency and comfort are important.

PALMA RANGE 25MM HONEYCOMB BLINDS

The Palma Range 25mm Honeycomb Blind offers a slim, elegant profile with excellent insulation properties. The compact cellular structure helps reduce heat transfer while providing a stylish and practical window covering solution.

Perfect for bedrooms, offices and living areas where a clean appearance, privacy and improved comfort are required.

PALMA RANGE 45MM HONEYCOMB BLINDS

The Palma Range 45mm Honeycomb Blind provides a larger cellular structure for enhanced insulation and superior temperature control.

Designed for larger windows and spaces requiring maximum comfort, these blinds offer a premium look while helping manage sunlight, glare and indoor temperatures.
