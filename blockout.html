BLOCKOUT ROLLER BLINDS

Privacy, Comfort & Elegant Light Control

Blockout roller blinds are designed for areas where maximum privacy and reduced sunlight are important. The specially coated fabric helps block outside light while creating a comfortable and stylish indoor environment.

Perfect for bedrooms, living rooms, offices and entertainment areas, blockout roller blinds help protect furniture and interiors from harsh sunlight while adding a modern finishing touch.

As part of our Roller Blind collection, Blockout fabrics are available with both manual operation and motorized control options for effortless everyday use.
