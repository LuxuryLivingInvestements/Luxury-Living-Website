ABOUT LUXURY LIVING

At Luxury Living - Swakopmund, we believe every home deserves the perfect balance of beauty, comfort and functionality. We specialise in premium window coverings and interior solutions designed for Namibia's unique coastal environment.

From elegant Roller Blinds and Honeycomb Blinds to Venetian Blinds, Vertical Blinds, Elite SQ Curtain Rails and a complete range of luxury shutters, every installation is custom made to complement your home.

Whether you are building a new home, renovating or completing a luxury development, our experienced team provides professional advice, accurate measuring, quality products and precision installation from start to finish.
